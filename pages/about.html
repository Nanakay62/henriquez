Our Story

Rooted in Sterling, Growing Across Virginia

Who We Are

Born from a Passion for Beautiful Spaces

Every great landscape begins with a vision — and Henriquez Landscaping began with exactly that. Born from a deep love of the outdoors and a genuine belief that every home deserves a yard to be proud of, we planted our roots right here in Sterling, Virginia over a decade ago and never looked back.

What started as a one-man mission with a mower and a dream has blossomed into Northern Virginia's most trusted landscaping team. We've had the privilege of transforming hundreds of properties — from modest starter homes to grand estates — each one treated with the same unwavering care and attention to detail.

We don't just cut grass. We craft outdoor experiences. We sculpt spaces where families gather, where children play, and where memories are made under the open Virginia sky. Our philosophy is simple: your outdoor space should feel like an extension of who you are — and we pour everything we have into making that vision real.

Grounded in eco-friendly practices and powered by genuine community pride, we're not just a landscaping company — we're your neighbors, and this land is as precious to us as it is to you.
