Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Henriquez Landscaping ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or contact us for services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website or contact us:

Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and property address when you submit an estimate request or contact form.

Usage Information: General information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited and time spent on the site. This is collected anonymously and used only to improve our website experience.

Photos: If you voluntarily upload property photos as part of your estimate request, these are used solely to assess your landscaping needs and prepare your estimate.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to: respond to your estimate requests and inquiries, provide the landscaping services you've requested, communicate with you about your project, and improve our website and services. We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties.

3. Communications

By submitting a contact or estimate form, you consent to receive communications from Henriquez Landscaping via phone, email, or text message regarding your inquiry. You may opt out at any time by replying "STOP" to any text message or emailing us at henriquezlandscaping@outlook.com.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us via phone if you have concerns about sharing sensitive information online.

5. Third-Party Services

Our website may use Google Maps for location embedding. Use of Google Maps is governed by Google's own Privacy Policy. We do not share your personal data with Google beyond what is technically required for the map functionality to work.

6. Cookies

Our website may use basic session cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies do not collect personal information and expire when you close your browser. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us at henriquezlandscaping@outlook.com or call 571-691-2176.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
