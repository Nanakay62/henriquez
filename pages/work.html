Lawn Care & Maintenance

A lush, healthy lawn doesn't happen by accident — it's the result of expert care, the right timing, and a deep understanding of Virginia's climate. Our comprehensive lawn programs are tailored to your grass type and property.

  • Weekly & bi-weekly mowing
  • Precision edging & trimming
  • Fertilization programs
  • Core aeration & overseeding
  • Weed & pest control
  • Soil testing & amendment

Smart Irrigation Systems

Water smarter, not more. Our irrigation systems deliver the right amount of water to the right places at the right time — keeping your landscape thriving while reducing water waste and utility bills.

  • Full system design & installation
  • Smart controller upgrades
  • Drip irrigation for beds
  • Spring startup & winterization
  • Leak detection & repair
  • Water-use optimization
